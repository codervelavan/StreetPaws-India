Step 1
Resident opens camera
Any resident taps the camera button. GPS is automatically recorded. No account setup needed — just a phone number via OTP.
↓
Step 2
AI processes the photo
Server checks image quality, detects the dog, extracts a 128-number visual fingerprint, and searches all existing profiles for a match.
↓
Step 3
Dog profile updated
If matched (>92% confidence), the existing dog's sighting history is updated. If no match, a new dog profile is created automatically.
↓
Step 4
NGO capture team notified
The dog appears on the NGO dashboard map as a priority capture target. Unneutered dogs in high-density zones are ranked first.
